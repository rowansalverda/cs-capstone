AUVIC 2026 Team

AUVIC is a student team at the University of Victoria dedicated to designing, building, and improving autonomous underwater vehicles. Our members collaborate across mechanical, software, and electrical systems to develop vehicles for competition, hands-on learning, and innovative underwater problem-solving. The team works together on design, testing, research, troubleshooting, and team operations throughout the year.

Our Sponsors

AUVIC is grateful for the organizations that support our team, our projects, and student innovation. Our sponsors play an important role in helping AUVIC design, build, and improve autonomous underwater vehicles. Learn more about the companies and organizations that support our work.

BC Ferries

BC Ferries is one of the largest ferry operators in the world, connecting coastal communities across British Columbia through passenger and vehicle ferry services.

CEWIL Canada

CEWIL Canada supports work-integrated learning across the country, helping students gain practical, career-building experience through education and industry partnerships.

Rainhouse Manufacturing Canada Ltd.

Rainhouse Manufacturing provides advanced manufacturing and machining services, helping create high-quality custom components for engineering and technology projects.

Altium

Altium develops electronics design software used for printed circuit board design, supporting engineers and teams working on complex electrical systems.

Altech Anodizing Ltd.

Altech Anodizing provides metal finishing and anodizing services, helping improve the durability, performance, and appearance of precision components.

SendCutSend

SendCutSend offers fast custom manufacturing services including laser cutting, bending, and fabrication for teams building prototypes and engineered parts.

Interested in Supporting AUVIC?

Sponsorship helps our team continue developing vehicles, competing, and giving students valuable hands-on experience in engineering and design.
